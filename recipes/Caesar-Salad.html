Caesar Salad

Description:

Caesar Salad is a popular and refreshing dish made with crisp romaine lettuce, crunchy croutons, Parmesan cheese, and a creamy Caesar dressing. Originally created in Mexico by Italian chef Caesar Cardini, this salad has become a worldwide favorite known for its rich and tangy flavor.

The secret to a delicious Caesar Salad lies in its dressing, which combines garlic, lemon juice, olive oil, egg yolk, Worcestershire sauce, and cheese to create a bold and creamy texture. It can be served as a light meal or as a side dish alongside grilled chicken, seafood, or pasta.

Ingredients:

  • 1 large head romaine lettuce, chopped
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp Dijon mustard
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Optional: grilled chicken or shrimp

Steps:

  1. Wash and dry the romaine lettuce thoroughly, then chop it into bite-sized pieces.
  2. In a bowl, prepare the dressing by whisking together garlic, Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, Dijon mustard, and egg yolk.
  3. Slowly drizzle in olive oil while whisking continuously until the dressing becomes creamy and smooth.
  4. Add grated Parmesan cheese and mix well. Season with salt and black pepper.
  5. Place the chopped lettuce in a large bowl and pour the dressing over it.
  6. Toss the lettuce gently until evenly coated with the dressing.
  7. Add croutons and extra Parmesan cheese on top.
  8. Serve immediately, optionally topping with grilled chicken or shrimp.
